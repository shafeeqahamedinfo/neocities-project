About the Platform

What is Neocities?

Neocities is a free web hosting service that brings back the creative spirit of the early web. It's a platform where developers, artists, and creators can build unique websites without corporate constraints.

Why Neocities?

In an age of social media platforms and template-based websites, Neocities stands as a beacon for creative freedom. It's a place where you can truly express yourself through code and design.

Every project showcased here represents hours of creative coding, problem-solving, and the joy of building something from scratch. From simple calculators to complex games, each one tells a story.

Project Philosophy

  • Every project is built from scratch with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Focus on functionality, creativity, and user experience
  • Open source mindset - learn, share, and inspire others
  • Continuous learning through hands-on project development
